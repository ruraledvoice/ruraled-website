Our Mission

RuralEd Voice is committed to transforming rural education by addressing the core barriers that keep children from learning. We focus on building digital capacity, providing safe transport to school, delivering essential learning materials, and introducing low-tech learning solutions like phone-based tutoring. By reaching learners where they are, we ensure every child has a fair chance to thrive through education.

Our Programs

  • ICT Labs for Rural Schools: Equipping schools with digital tools and access to 21st-century learning.
  • Learning Through Phone Calls: Bringing lessons directly to learners with no internet access using basic phones.
  • School Transportation: Providing safe, school-managed transport solutions to reduce lateness and exhaustion.
  • Basic School Materials: Supplying uniforms, books, and writing materials to learners in need.
  • Teaching & Learning Resources: Supporting rural teachers and students with curriculum-aligned content and tools.
