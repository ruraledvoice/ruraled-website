About RuralEd Voice

RuralEd Voice is an education-focused initiative supporting underprivileged learners across Ghana’s rural communities. Our mission is to bridge the educational gap through transportation, ICT access, teaching and learning materials, and phone-based learning.

Meet the Founder

Shaban Tia Luri is a basic school teacher from Silbelle, a rural village in Ghana. With a Bachelor of Education (B.Ed), he is widely recognized for his unwavering dedication to supporting disadvantaged learners. Born on January 6, 1998, Shaban has initiated several grassroots solutions to help learners thrive — from school transport to ICT labs — all powered by community spirit and innovation.
